## Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and productive environment for all authorized team members and contributors working on Revive Roots Essentials. All participants are expected to uphold high standards of conduct, integrity, and professionalism.

## Expected Conduct

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive team environment include:

- Using clear, professional, and inclusive language in all communications
- Being respectful of diverse opinions, perspectives, and experiences
- Providing constructive feedback in a respectful manner
- Accepting criticism gracefully and acting on it professionally
- Prioritizing the success and security of the project
- Showing respect and professionalism towards all team members
- Respecting confidentiality of proprietary code and business information
- Adhering to licensing terms and intellectual property agreements

## Unacceptable Conduct

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Violating confidentiality or sharing proprietary code without authorization
- Disrespectful, abusive, or hostile language or behavior
- Personal attacks, harassment, or intimidation
- Unwelcome advances of any kind
- Publishing private information without permission
- Attempting to reverse engineer, decompile, or circumvent licensing controls
- Sublicensing, reselling, or redistributing code without written authorization
- Using code or derivative works in projects beyond the licensed scope
- Deliberately disrupting work or communications
- Any form of discrimination or harassment

## Critical Licensing Compliance

All authorized contributors must:

- Respect and maintain all intellectual property protections
- Never distribute, sublicense, or resell the software without explicit written permission
- Comply with the terms outlined in the executed commercial agreement
- Report any suspected unauthorized access or licensing violations
- Store all credentials and secrets securely and never commit them to version control
- Use materials only within the scope authorized by the license agreement

## Enforcement and Resolution

Violations of this Code of Conduct will result in:

1. Immediate review by project leadership
2. Confidential investigation of the incident
3. Direct communication with the violating party
4. Remediation requirements, suspension, or termination of access as appropriate
5. Potential legal action in cases of licensing violations or IP theft

Serious violations—including unauthorized distribution, licensing circumvention, or confidentiality breaches—may result in:

- Immediate revocation of access rights
- Termination of the commercial agreement
- Legal action to recover damages and enforce intellectual property rights
- Referral to law enforcement
